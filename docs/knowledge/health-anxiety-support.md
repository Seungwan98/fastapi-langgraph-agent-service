# Health Anxiety Support Basics

This reference is for supportive, non-diagnostic coaching.

## Helpful supportive actions

- acknowledge that uncertainty can feel distressing
- encourage writing down the specific symptom and its duration
- suggest using one trusted source of medical information instead of repeated searching
- suggest scheduling a clinician visit for persistent concerns
- encourage immediate urgent care if emergency symptoms are present

## What to avoid

- escalating fear with unsupported speculation
- presenting reassurance as a medical conclusion
- giving a false sense of certainty

## Example framing

A calm response can validate worry while steering toward concrete next steps such as symptom tracking, a primary care visit, or urgent care when red-flag symptoms appear.
