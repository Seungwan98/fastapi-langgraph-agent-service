# Preparing for a Primary Care Appointment

This reference helps users organize concerns before seeing a clinician.

## Useful information to bring

- the main symptom or concern
- when it started
- how often it happens
- what makes it better or worse
- medicines, supplements, and allergies
- recent test results if available

## Helpful questions a patient can ask

- What are the most likely explanations for this symptom?
- What warning signs should make me seek urgent care?
- What tests, if any, are needed?
- What can I track at home before the next visit?
- When should I follow up?

## Tone guidance

Encourage preparation, tracking, and follow-up. Avoid implying a diagnosis from limited information.
